Summer Split

he Summer split takes place between the months of July and September. The teams around the world battle in a multiple week tournament, which culminates in Summer Playoffs and then Summer Finals. The winner of Summer Finals not only gets the Summer Split Trophy, but also a direct qualification to the second international tournament of the year, the World Championship.

The regular part of the split usually consists of "Best of 1 matches", where each of the teams meets all of their opponents 2 times. After all the Bo1s have been played. The Playoffs begin. There, the teams are seeded into a bracket, where the first team plays a match against the worst qualified team, and so on. The matches are Best of 5.
