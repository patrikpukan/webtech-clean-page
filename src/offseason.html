The offseason

The offseason between the League of Legends World Championship and the Spring Split is a time for teams to rest, regroup, and prepare for the upcoming season. During this period, teams can make roster changes, sign new players, and participate in various tournaments and events 1. The offseason typically lasts for a few months, with the exact duration varying from region to region. For instance, in North America, the LCS Spring Split officially kicks off on January 26, 2023, and runs for two months 2. In contrast, the LCK Spring Split in South Korea starts on January 5, 2023, and runs for three months 3. During the offseason, teams can participate in various tournaments and events, such as the All-Star Event, which features the best players from each region competing against each other.
