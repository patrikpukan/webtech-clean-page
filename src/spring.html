Spring Split

The Spring split takes place between the months of January and April. The teams around the world battle in a multiple week tournament, which culminates in Spring Playoffs and then Spring Finals. The winner of Spring Finals not only gets the Spring Split Trophy, but also a direct qualification to the first international tournament of the year, the Mid Season Invitational.
